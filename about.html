Milestone Projects

Sign Language Translation System

This project is close to my heart. Using JavaScript, Ionic, and MySQL, I built a system that translates sign language into Pedi, English, and Zulu. It’s not just code—it’s a bridge to connect communities and make communication accessible for all.

Open Lab Management System

Born out of a need to streamline university lab bookings, this database-driven system optimizes student access to resources. It’s a practical solution that makes academic life smoother and more efficient.

Software Development Internship at ICEP

During my internship, I dove into building user interfaces and optimizing databases with React.js, Node.js, and JavaScript. Working in a collaborative team, I learned the power of integrating APIs to create seamless, user-focused solutions.

3G Administrator at African Bank

As a 3G Administrator, I managed mobile data operations and tackled issues head-on, improving processes by 15% through clear, streamlined guidelines. It taught me how tech can drive real-world efficiency.

Beyond Technology

Tech is my canvas, but people are my inspiration. As a Deputy Presiding Officer for the IEC, I led with precision, ensuring a seamless voting process for over 700 voters. That role honed my leadership and communication skills, showing me how to connect technical expertise with human impact. I love collaborating, simplifying complex ideas, and empowering others through training.
